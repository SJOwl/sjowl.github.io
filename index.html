Swipe Gestures Launcher Privacy Policy

Swipe Gestures Launcher uses an accessibility service to help Users with disabilities to perform custom actions by simple swipe on the screen.

Swipe Gestures Launcher also uses some sensitive permissions as fast shortcuts that will be performed by the User only.

Swipe Gestures Launcher does not collect any kind of information and does not share any information with third party.
